Welcome!

This is a website where we keep our recipes and organize our meal planning. It is very much a work in progress, and has only been alive for a couple months, but it's coming around!

What can I do here?

If you have an account, go to your planner, and you can start laying out your own monthly meal plans. If not, you can still check out the full recipe list and pick something out for dinner. If you just want to learn a bit more about the site, check the about page.

Anything else?

You can also look at the monthly plans Cait has made! The mobile experience is coming along, and should keep getting better.

What about my own plans?

To make your own calendars, you'll need to have an account to login. If you don't have one, just ask her and we can get you one once we set this all up.
