About this Website!

This website began development in 2018 as a way to accomplish three goals: have a single place to store our family recipes that Cait develops and adapts, have a way to plan out monthly meals and share these plans with anyone interested, and finally develop a codebase into which my kids can contribute. This last reason was the driving force behind creating our own website instead of using one of the existing recipe/meal planning sites (though with those we'd be on a free plan and have to deal with limited functionality or ads...).

I have had people ask if they can have their own accounts on here, and it's definitely getting closer. I need to make sure the recipe creation/edit pages are set up nicely and work out the relationships with users accessing their own and other users recipes. Once that is complete (a couple weeks) and it should be good to go for a few people.

If you find a bug in the site, good job! If you are visiting this site, you are likely a friend of ours, so just let us know personally. If you don't know us personally, glad you are here! Please use a Github account to file an issue on the repository listed below!

Other Information

In my opinion, recipes are a free-form expression of taste and creativity. There can be a million people making the same recipe and make it different each time, putting their own influence on it. That said, there are people who make a living out of coming up with, or providing, recipes. We have zero interest in interfering with that venture. This website is not a money making venture...at all. If you notice a recipe on this site that you own, and you feel it is your intellectual property, just let us know, and we'll take it down. We'll still keep it in our beautiful recipe box in the real life kitchen drawer, and we might even change the ingredients/directions to instead be a link to your page where you provide the recipe! But just remember, the same recipe for boiled eggs can be found on about a million websites right now, so the recipe on this page could have been simply a creative coincidence!

And finally, thanks for visiting!!
